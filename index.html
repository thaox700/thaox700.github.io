About Us

Your trusted electrical partner in St. Paul

At One Electrics and Engineers LLC, we bring years of experience and dedication to every electrical project we undertake. Based in St. Paul, Minnesota, we're a locally-owned and operated business committed to providing the highest quality electrical services to homeowners and businesses throughout the Twin Cities area.

Our team of licensed and insured electricians takes pride in delivering safe, reliable, and code-compliant electrical solutions. Whether you're upgrading your home's electrical panel, installing an EV charger, or need commercial electrical work, we approach every job with the same level of professionalism and attention to detail.

We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients through honest communication, fair pricing, and exceptional workmanship. When you choose One Electrics and Engineers LLC, you're choosing a partner who cares about your safety and satisfaction.
